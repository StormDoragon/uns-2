Family · Qur’an 25:74

Coolness of the Eyes at Home

A prayer for family, marriage, children, and a home that helps you walk toward Allah.

The dua

رَبَّنَا هَبْ لَنَا مِنْ أَزْوَاجِنَا وَذُرِّيَّاتِنَا قُرَّةَ أَعْيُنٍ وَاجْعَلْنَا لِلْمُتَّقِينَ إِمَامًا

Rabbanā hab lanā min azwājinā wa dhurriyyātinā qurrata a‘yūnin waj‘alnā lil-muttaqīna imāmā

“Our Lord, grant us from our spouses and descendants comfort to our eyes, and make us examples for the mindful.”

Qur’an 25:74

The Relatable Pain

Home can be the softest place in your life, or the place where your heart feels most tested. Marriage, parenting, longing for family, worrying over children, healing from family wounds — these are not small matters. They sit close to the soul.

This dua is for the person who does not only want a family that looks good from the outside. They want a home where the eyes cool, the heart rests, and Allah is remembered.

The Dark Place

The Qur’an places this dua among the qualities of the servants of the Most Merciful. Their private hopes are not shallow. They ask Allah to make their spouses and descendants a source of qurrat a‘yun — coolness of the eyes.

That phrase carries relief after heat, tears turning into calm, and the kind of joy that does not agitate the heart. It is the opposite of a home that constantly burns you out.

The Turning Point

The dua does not stop at comfort. It rises into responsibility: وَاجْعَلْنَا لِلْمُتَّقِينَ إِمَامًا — make us leaders for the mindful. In other words, make our home not only soothing, but righteous; not only loving, but guiding.

This is a mature family dua. It asks for tenderness and taqwa together. It asks Allah to make the people closest to you a reason your faith becomes steadier, not weaker.

The Closure

Not every family story is simple. Some people recite this dua with gratitude, some with fear, some with longing, and some with grief. The beauty is that all of those hearts can still ask Allah to place coolness where there has been heat.

Whisper it for your spouse, your children, your future family, your parents, or your own wounded sense of home. Ask Allah to build a household that your eyes can rest in and your akhirah can benefit from.

The takeaway for you

A beautiful home is not only peaceful; it helps the people inside it become beloved to Allah.

Give the words space. Read them once for meaning, once for need, and once as a quiet return to Allah.
