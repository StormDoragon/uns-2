If you do not know where to begin

Start with a three-dua rhythm.

One dua for trust, one for forgiveness, and one for what you are asking Allah to open.

  1. Ground the heart. Say ḥasbunallāhu wa ni‘mal-wakīl and name what you are entrusting to Allah.
  2. Return with honesty. Say the dua of Yunus عليه السلام without pretending you are unaffected.
  3. Ask with need. Say the dua of Musa عليه السلام for the good only Allah can send.
